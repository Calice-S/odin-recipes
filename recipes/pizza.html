Pizza

Description

As I was looking out the window, reminiscing about the past year, the idea came to me to share my favorite New York style pizza so others can feel the joy that I don't feel this year. If you're wondering why I don't feel in the spirits this christmas season is because my husband left me for the neighbor, I can hear them laughing as I type. My cat also left me, she gave me one last look and never came home from one of her walks, I suspect she's also at the neighbor's house. Anyway, onto the pizza the real reason why you're here, not to hear my troubles.

Ingredients

  • 22 1/2 ounces bread flour, plus more for dusting
  • 0.5 ounce granulated sugar
  • 0.35 ounce kosher salt
  • 0.35 ounce instant yeast
  • 15 ounces lukewarm water
  • 3 tablespoons
  • 1 batch of New York Style Pizza Sauce
  • 1 pound grated full-fat dry mozzarella cheese, placed in freezer for at least 15 minutes
